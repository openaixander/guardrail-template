The Mechanism

Three guardrails.
Absolute financial clarity.

Step 01

Lock the Foundation

Begin by declaring your monthly income and every fixed, non-negotiable obligation — rent, loan repayments, subscriptions. These are locked and untouchable. What remains is your true discretionary pool.

Step 02

The Silent Buffer

Guardrail silently skims 15% of your discretionary pool into a hidden emergency vault before you ever see it. This friction-free cushion absorbs real-world price spikes, medical surprises, and inflation shocks — invisibly.

Step 03

Dynamic Calibration

The remaining cash is divided by the exact number of days left in your billing cycle. Each midnight, the engine recalculates. You receive one clean number — your active daily constraint — and nothing else.

The Formula

Income − Fixed Costs − 15% Buffer ÷ Days Remaining = Your Daily Limit
